Welcome to Temple Inn and Suites!

We are a specialty hotel chain catering to the needs of members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints who come to serve in the temple or are participating in events such as sealings, weddings, receptions, and youth trips.

If you are not a church member, you are welcome to stay with us, providing you're willing to abide by church standards, in particular no drinking or smoking on the property.

The Gilbert, Arizona Temple

Located at 3303 S Greenfield Rd in Gilbert, directly across the street from our hotel. Announced April 10, 2008, the groundbreaking was November 13, 2010, and it was dedicated March 2, 2014. See the temples page for hours, closures, and contact information.

The Gilbert temple has clothing rental available. There is no food service in the temple itself, but with our restaurant so near, you won't go hungry. The distribution center is about a mile down the street, and our shuttle-van will gladly take you there.
