Reservations

We have several room types:

  • Single: One queen-sized bed - max 2 people - $39/night
  • Double: Two queen-sized beds - max 4 people - $59/night
  • Super: Two king-sixed beds - max 4 adults or 2 adults & 4 children - $69/night
  • We have a limited number of handicapped-accessible rooms. If you need one, please state it in the reservation form.
    • Specialty Room Types:
    • Dorm: We have two dorm rooms with 14 twin-sized bunk beds for use by youth groups. Please call 1-800-867-5309 to check availablity and to reserve. $99/night
    • Missionary Suite: We have 10 missionary suites with one queen bed and a kitchenette for the use of full-time temple missionaries. Please call 1-800-867-5309 for availablity and to reserve. $99/week
