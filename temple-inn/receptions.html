Wedding Receptions

Each of our facilities has a conference room available for wedding receptions and other meetings. The room sizes and occupancy varies by location. The maximum occupancy at our smallest facility is 300. We can accommodate chairs in rows for meetings, and for weddings, round tables that seat eight.

Tables and chairs

For weddings, we have circular tables and padded folding chairs. We also have rectangular buffet tables for serving food.

For meetings, we have a lectern with a microphone and will fill the room with padded folding chairs.

Linens

We have round, white tablecloths that fit our tables. We also have white linens with clear plastic covers for our buffet tables.

Dishes

We have clear plates and glasses, and flatware available for rent. We can also provide plastic tableware, or the guests cam provide their own.

Sound

There are speakers embedded in the ceiling throughout the room, which may be used by a DJ, the integrated five-disc CD player, or the lecturn microphone.

Decorations

Any decorations must be provided by the guest.

Food Preparation and Service

We have a small kitchen available for the preparation and heating of food. Guests are welcome to use the kitchen themselves, have a caterer use it, or hire our catering staff.

Food

Our catering staff has several simple menus to choose from, or you can make your own arrangements to have the food brought in by the wedding party.

Wedding Cake

Our staff can provide sheet cakes. Tiered cakes must be arranged for by the guest. Due to their complexity, tiered cakes may not be prepared on site.

Cost

To keep costs down, each item used by guests is calculated separately, eg, room rental, table and chair set-up, table setting, dish rental, and so forth. Call the reception planner at your facility to discuss facilities, availability, and prices.
